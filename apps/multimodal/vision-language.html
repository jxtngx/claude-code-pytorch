About Vision-Language Models

This interface demonstrates multimodal AI capabilities using state-of-the-art vision-language models like CLIP, ALIGN, and BLIP. These models learn joint representations of images and text through contrastive learning.

Key Features:

• Zero-shot classification without training on specific classes
• Cross-modal retrieval between images and text
• Multi-lingual understanding capabilities
• 224x224 image resolution with 77-token text limit
• Contrastive learning for robust multimodal embeddings
